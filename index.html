Benchmarking Ribotagger

We compare the following competing softwares against Ribotagger using a common dataset - Anoxic aerobic tanks from Ulu Pandan wastewater treatment plant for the V4 region.

  1. SSUSearch - Microbial community analysis with ribosomal gene fragments from shotgun metagenomes (Guo et al 2016)
    • Pubmed
    • Software
  2. riboFrame from Ramazzotti et al 2015
    • Pubmed
    • Software

others (but not investigated)

  • Metaxa and Metaxa2. Work from Bentsson et al.
    • Pubmed - Metaxa
    • Pubmed - Metaxa2 Bengtsson-Palme et al. 2015
    • Software
